Executive Summary

Option A: Precise Internal Audit

A rigorous, data-heavy approach utilizing actual project logs.

  • High Accuracy
  • Requires weeks of data mining

Option B: Industry Benchmarks

A rapid strategic assessment applying standard industry ratios.

  • Immediate Results
  • Best for Strategic Review

Digital & Retention

Loss of institutional knowledge.

Method 1: Precise Internal Audit

Calculates exact financial loss by auditing internal records.

  • Data Source: Timesheets, VO Logs.
  • Best For: Justifying software budget.

Method 1: Calculation Models

1. Non-Productive Time

Loss = Hourly Rate × Wasted Hours × Staff

2. Cost of Quality

Loss = Σ (Site Rework Costs + Design VOs)

Method 2: Industry Benchmarks

Applies standard ratios (CII, CIPD) to estimate exposure.

  • Data Source: Project Values, Payroll.
  • Best For: Strategic restructuring.

The "Admin Drag"

Risks: ED-02, ED-10

High-value engineers spend time on manual data entry.

Benchmark Impact: 30% of Payroll wasted.

The "Rework Tax"

Risks: ED-05, ED-06

Bypassing reviews leads to site errors.

Benchmark Impact: 5% of Contract Value at risk.

The "Turnover Penalty"

Risks: ED-04, ED-07

High attrition leads to expensive outsourcing.

Benchmark Impact: Replacement costs 150% of salary.
