Detailed Breakdown

Analysis of specific risk categories and their benchmarks.

1. The "Rework Tax"

ED-03 ED-05 ED-06

Benchmark: 5% of Total Contract Value

Work proceeding without engineering approval, scope gaps, and unrealistic schedules creates a direct financial penalty. Industry studies (CII) indicate 52% of rework is attributed to poor document management and data communication.

2. The "Admin Drag"

ED-02 ED-09 ED-10

Benchmark: 30% of Engineering Payroll

Highly paid engineers doing data entry, chasing IT for laptops, and manual document control. Lean Construction principles highlight that in unoptimized environments, value-adding engineering work drops below 50%.

3. The "Turnover Penalty"

ED-04 ED-07 ED-08

Benchmark: 50% of Annual Salary per Exit

Costs include recruitment agency fees, visa/onboarding downtime, and the "ramp-up" period where new hires operate at partial efficiency. Loss of institutional knowledge (ED-07) compounds this cost.
